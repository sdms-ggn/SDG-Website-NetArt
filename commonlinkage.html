Common Linkage




Cause (SDG 8) and effect (SDG 11)

How: Migration from rural to urban areas due to labour intensive or disguised unemployment or for any other reasons, leads to increased pressure on the comparatively developed cities.




When people do find a job there, it leads to further urbanisation, which is the ultimate adverse effect.




This further creates pressure on environment, other services such as public transport, banking etc. and leads to a kind of vicious cycle.
